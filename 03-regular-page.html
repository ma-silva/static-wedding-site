Frequently Asked Questions


Do we really need to RSVP?

YES. This is to guarantee you are included in the final guest list. If you come without prior confirmation, consider bringing your own chair and sandwich.

We already confirmed "YES" to the RSVP, but we cannot attend anymore due to certain circumstances, what will we do?

It is sad that you can no longer be part of our special day. Should this happen, please let us know as soon as possible so we can give your allotted seat/s to someone else.

Can I bring someone else with me?

We request that only those who are invited will come. Kindly check your invitation for alloted seats.

Limited budget + Limited Space = Limited guests.
