Frequently Asked Questions


What time will the ceremony start?

Processional march will strictly start at 1:30 PM. Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the processional march starts.

#NoToBetterLateThanNever

Do I really need to RSVP? I already confirmed via personal message.

Thank you so much for confirming! That was the first step which helped us draft our initial guest list. The RSVP is our second step to confirm you haven’t changed your mind. This will ensure you are included in the final guest list.

Can I bring someone else with me?

Unfortunately, NO. Please check your invitation for allotted seats.

Limited budget + Limited space = Limited guests

Can I bring kids?

This is going to be an ADULT-ONLY event and the only children attending are part of our entourage. We want guests to enjoy the evening and we hope you see this as an opportunity to celebrate with us and to fully enjoy it without any fuss.

I said “YES” to the RSVP but change of plans – I can’t come. What will I do?

Please let us know as soon as possible so we can give your allotted seat to someone else.

I said “NO” to the RSVP or I have not confirmed on FB events (seenzoned). I can attend now. What should I do?

As much as we want you to come, we already took your NO or SEEN as final confirmation. Please understand that we already allotted the seat to someone else when you declined (or refused to confirm) our invitation.

We can still meet you some other time after the wedding to catch up. Perhaps for lunch / dinner/ tea / coffee?

What time will the reception end?

Since it is a day before Christmas eve, we aim to end by 9:00 PM. We hope you stay until the end of the program. Please DON’T eat and run.

Can I take photos and videos?

During the wedding rites, we respectfully ask everyone to refrain from using their gadgets. We want to keep the solemnity of the event and the greatest gift you can give is your presence. Don’t worry, you can take all the photos and videos after the ceremony and during the reception.

How to get there?

You may use Waze or Google Maps, or any apps you are comfortable with. Just type in “St. Michael the Archangel Parish” for the ceremony and “The Atrium at Enderun” for the reception.

What can I wear?

You would all look wonderful in formal attire.

For the ladies, Long Dress, Cocktail Dress, or Dressy Separates

For the gents, Coat & Tie; and/or Collared Long Sleeves

Strictly NO wearing of T-shirts, jeans, shorts, slippers, sneakers, and rubber shoes.

How can we help the couple have the best time during their wedding day?

RSVP now!

Be there ON TIME.

Stay until the END of the program.

ENJOY!
